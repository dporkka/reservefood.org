## How long does it last?

Reserve Food bars are designed for multi-year ambient storage. Shelf life is validated through controlled quality and stability testing.

---

## Does it require refrigeration?

No. The product is designed to remain stable at room temperature.

---

## Is it meant to replace all food?

No. Reserve Food is designed as dependable nutrition for reserves, disruptions, and daily backup use.

---

## Can it be eaten daily?

Yes. The formulation is designed for repeat consumption with predictable tolerance.

---

## Is it suitable for large reserves?

Yes. Packaging, shelf life, and logistics are designed specifically for storage and rotation.
